General resources for grad school and life

Graduate school is not easy. Life is not easy. Here are some of my favorite websites/things that have helped me through it all.

You can find my GRFP advice and statements here!

Graduate School:

  • Connected papers: explore related articles via a cool visual graph
  • Astrosites: make your own website through this tutorial
  • While I was a big fan of paper to-do lists, a few fellow grads (hi Jen!) swore by using a Kanban Board!
  • Toggl: easy time tracking to see where your time goes
  • CV template on overleaf, forked from Dr. Ed Brown (MSU)
  • UConn PhD Thesis Template, adapted from the thesis of Dr. H Perry Hatchfield
  • Saving up for a nice pair of noise canceling headphones was a life-saver in those shared offices and on flights to conferences. Took a few paychecks, but was worth it.

Reading lists for being a responsible scientist, especially those that engage with machine learning and AI:

  • Weapons of Math Destruction by Dr. Cathy O'Neil
  • Race After Technology by Dr. Ruha Benjamin
  • Automating Inequity by Dr. Virginia Eubanks
  • Algorithms of Oppression by Dr. Safiya Umoja Noble
  • Viral Justice by Dr. Ruha Benjamin
  • This list is not exhaustive, please keep engaging with this material even when it makes you uncomfortable. Know of a book that should be here? Please let me know!
